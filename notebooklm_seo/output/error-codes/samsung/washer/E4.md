## Quick Summary

## Common Causes

1. Door not fully closed
2. Damaged door latch
3. Door switch failure
4. Debris in door seal

### Step 1: Close door firmly

### Step 2: Clean door seal

### Step 3: Replace door latch

### Step 4: Test door switch
