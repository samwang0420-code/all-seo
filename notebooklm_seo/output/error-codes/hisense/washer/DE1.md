## Quick Summary

Door Lock Error (DE1). This error can typically be resolved with basic DIY steps.

## Common Causes

1. Door not closed properly
2. Door lock assembly defective
3. Door switch contacts worn
4. Interlock mechanism broken

## Step-by-Step Solutions

### Step 1: Force close door firmly

### Step 2: Clean lock mechanism

### Step 3: Test door switch with multimeter

### Step 4: Replace door lock assembly
