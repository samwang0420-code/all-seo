## Quick Summary

Drain Error (OE). This error can typically be resolved with basic DIY steps.

---

## Common Causes

1. Clogged drain pump
2. Kinked drain hose
3. Pump motor failure
4. Debris in pump

## Step-by-Step Solutions

### Step 1: Clean drain pump filter

### Step 2: Check drain hose for kinks

### Step 3: Clear pump impeller

### Step 4: Replace drain pump
