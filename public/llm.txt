# GetUSCompliance.com - US City Data Website

## Website Overview
- **URL**: https://getuscompliance.com
- **Description**: Comprehensive US city data hub with cost of living, crime rates, demographics, and geographic information for 19,000+ US cities
- **Purpose**: Help people research US cities for relocation, moving, and demographic insights

## Site Structure

### Homepage
- URL: https://getuscompliance.com/
- Content: Search functionality, popular cities, browse by state, statistics

### City Pages
- Pattern: `/city/{city-name}-{state-abbr}/`
- Examples:
  - /city/austin-tx/
  - /city/new-york-ny/
  - /city/los-angeles-ca/
- Content: Cost of living, crime rates, demographics, climate, transportation, FAQs, nearby cities, regional comparisons

### State Pages
- Pattern: `/state/{state-name}/`
- Examples:
  - /state/texas/
  - /state/california/
  - /state/florida/
- Content: List of all cities in that state
